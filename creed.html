My Personal Creed

"I want to go on the adventure of my life.
Saying exactly what I think, relentlessly pursuing truth, pushing myself to the absolute limits of what I can do with the time I have.

I choose to become as powerful, competent, and useful as possible.
To voluntarily carry the heaviest burden I can bear, leaving nothing behind.

When the clock hits zero, the tank will be empty.
I'll have used every unfair advantage, smiled in the face of unfairness without bitterness, and focused ruthlessly on giving everything I possibly could, by choice."
